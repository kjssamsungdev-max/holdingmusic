How it works

A three-sided platform built for the people who actually make and play the music.

For Artists

Upload free. Get heard. Get paid.

If your tools include AI, your art still belongs to you. We surface your tracks to real venues and producers. We don’t take rights. We pay when your music wins.

  • City, country, genre & global leaderboards
  • 15% of net platform profit paid quarterly
  • 2% finder’s fee on signings (Trail Clause)
Become a founding artist
For Venues

A catalog that’s actually new.

Stream curated AI-music playlists tuned to the time of day, the room, the mood. Pay per venue, not per stream. Every play credits the artist who made it.

  • Genre, mood & daypart playlists
  • Single flat fee — no per-stream surprises
  • Embed leaderboards on your venue page
Bring HoldingMusic to your venue
For Labels & A&R

Where the next ones are coming from.

Scout artists by velocity, geography, and genre on a live leaderboard. Sign them direct. We take a small finder’s fee — no rights, no catalogs, no friction.

  • Rising Star list by velocity
  • Drill into city & venue breakdowns
  • Direct artist contact, transparent fees
